Numbers 100+


100=a hundred; 200=two hundred; 800=eight hundred.
1000= a thousand; 3000= three thousand. Note there is no 's' on the end of 'hundred', 'thousand' or 'million'.
To say big numbers in English, we start from the left and say the biggest numbers first, e.g.:
150=a/one hundred and fifty; 1,500=one thousand, five hundred; 1550=one thousand, five hundred and fifty.
10,579=ten thousand, five hundred and seventy nine;
2,345,678=two million, three hundred and forty-five thousand, six hundred and seventy- eight.
Note in English we use a comma to separate thousands.
In British English, we put 'and' after the hundreds. American English does not.
