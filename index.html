Language learning involves many skills such as listening, reading and grammar. With a limited vocabulary, many people could pass an A2 exam,
maybe even a B1 if their grammar was good enough. However, for those wishing to take their English to another level, vocabulary is vital.
Not to take anything away from grammar with its passives, conditionals and inverted nearly negative structures, but there are already so many
websites to practice grammar and so few to really delve deep into vocabulary use and more importantly, practice.
Whether you're preparing for an exam and hoping to show off your lexical range to the examiners or simply want to improve your own knowledge
in this language, vocabulary will go a long way to making you stand out from the rest.
