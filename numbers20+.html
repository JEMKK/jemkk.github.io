Numbers 20+


In English, we first say the tens and then the single digit, e.g.
- 31 is thirty-one;
- 79 is seventy-nine;
- 63 is sixty-three.
Notice the spelling differences, e.g.:
thirTEEN (13) and thirTY (30).
Note the hyphen(-) between the ten and the single digit.
